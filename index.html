Musicle

Inspired by the original chordle. Each chord starts between C#3 and C4 and extends a maximum of one octave.

How to play

Guess the chord in 5 tries

The chords can be in any inversion of any major, minor, 7th or augmented key

Use the flip button to change between enharmonics

The colour of the keys and tiles will change to show how close your guess was. For example:

C4 G4 E5 F5

The note C4 is the correct note in the correct position

The note G4 is in the chord, but not in this position

There is at least one E in the chord, but not in this octave

There are no Fs in the chord

In this case the chord was Cmaj: C4 E4 G4 C5
